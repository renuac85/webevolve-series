如何参会？
How can I participate?

本次会议支持现场参会（北京）与远程参会（线上）。
This meeting is hybrid with the main in-person hub in Beijing, and remote attendees.

会议注册 (Registration)：@@

欢迎于6月16日前进行注册。
Registration opens through 16 June.

注册成功后将于一周内收到参会确认邮件。远程接入信息将于会前两天单独发送至与会者注册时提供的邮箱地址。
Attendee will receive a confirmation email within one week after the registration. The remote participation information will be sent to the attedee's email address individually two days prior to the meeting.

会议免费面向 W3C 会员及受邀嘉宾开放。
This meeting is free and open to W3C Members and invited guests.
