如何参会？
How can I participate?

会议注册 (Registration)：@@

欢迎于6月16日前进行注册。
Registration opens through 16 June.

重要提示：请务必实名注册并携带身份证参会。会议现场办理参会通行证时，须确保注册时填写的与会者姓名与其身份证姓名完全一致。

NOTE: Please register using your real name and bring your ID card to the meeting. In order to get a meeting pass onsite, the registration name and the name on your ID card must be consistent.

注册成功后将于一周内收到参会确认邮件。
Attendee will receive a confirmation email within one week after the registration.

会议免费面向 W3C 会员及受邀嘉宾开放。
This meeting is free and open to W3C Members and invited guests.
