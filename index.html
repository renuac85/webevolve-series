Web 进化论 ｜ WebEvolve

“Web 进化论”是由一系列专题构成的开放论坛：关注并探讨 Web 领域最新热点技术话题，通过轻量级技术分享与讨论，促进围绕新技术的观点碰撞、探索 Web 技术标准需求、孵化潜在的标准化方向、推动标准落地实现等。“Web 进化论”系列专题活动的相关话题由行业与社区驱动。活动免费面向公众开放、线上线下形式不限。

"WebEvolve" is an open forum composed of a series of events on community-interested topics through introduction on the latest Web technologies, exchanging of ideas around emerging technologies, exploring the needs of new Web technology standards, promoting the implementations of standards, etc. The "WebEvolve" events are driven by W3C community, free and open to the public.
