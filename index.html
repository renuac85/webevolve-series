Web 进化论 ｜ WebEvolve

“Web 进化论”是由一系列专题构成的开放论坛：关注并探讨 Web 领域最新热点技术话题，通过轻量级技术分享与讨论，促进围绕新技术的观点碰撞、探索 Web 技术标准需求、孵化潜在的标准化方向、推动标准落地实现等。“Web 进化论”系列专题活动在行业及社区需求驱动下，由 W3C 成员主导相关讨论。活动免费面向公众开放、线上线下形式不限。

"WebEvolve" is an open forum composed of a series of events on various topics through lightweight dicussions focusing on the latest Web technologies, accelerating the exchange of ideas around emerging technoligies, exploring the needs of Web technology standards, incubating potential standardization directions, promoting the implementations on standards, etc. The "WebEvolve" series of thematic events is driven by industry and community needs and led by W3C members. The event is free and open to the public.
