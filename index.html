W3C MiniApps 标准落地与实现行业讨论会

Industry Meetup: W3C MiniApps Standardization and Implementation

由中国厂商牵头主导的 W3C 小程序/快应用标准 （MiniApps Specifications），目前几乎全部技术规范已经进入稳定阶段，进入相关审阅流程。W3C MiniApps 工作组即将启动测试套件的开发，W3C MiniApps 工作组成员单位也将开始小程序标准在产业的落地实施。在此背景下，MiniApps 工作组联合小程序/快应用相关厂商将于2023年12月22日在 W3C 中国总部所在地北京航空航天大学进行面对面沟通，讨论 MiniApps 技术规范以及其实现和部署相关的规划。

W3C MiniApps Working Group has been making considerable progress with the development of MiniApps Specifications. Most of the MiniApps specifications have been stabilized and ready for broader review. The W3C MiniApps Working Group will start working on testing and its participants start to prepare for implementation of MiniApps specifications. With this background, the W3C MiniApps Working Group plans to meet more MiniApps vendors, collect feedback from the industry and discuss the implementation plan on 22 December 2023 in Beihang University, Beijing.

注：为了便于更多厂商参与讨论，本次活动以中文为主，部分发言为英文。本次会议支持远程参会。

Note: to enable broader participation from the industry, the discussion will be mainly in Chinese and some talks will be in English. Remote participation will also be supported.
